📌 Company Overview

Logic & Gate is a sole proprietorship engaged in mobile application development and digital entertainment services. Founded in 16 April 2026 by Solayman Islam Ruman, the company focuses on building innovative play-to-earn mobile applications for the global Android market.

Our flagship product, Tanzanite Mining, is a mobile application where users earn virtual TAN points through mini-games, rewarded advertisements, and daily activities, which can be withdrawn as real Solana (SOL) cryptocurrency rewards.
