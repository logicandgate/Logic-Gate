Website Privacy Policy

Effective: May 16, 2026  ·  Last updated: May 16, 2026

At Logic & Gate, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy applies to our website (logicandgate.com) and explains that we do not collect any personal information from visitors.

✅  Short version: We collect zero personal data. No cookies, no tracking, no forms.

1. No Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or process any personal data from visitors to our website. You can browse our website completely anonymously.

2. No Cookies or Tracking Technologies

Our website does not use cookies, web beacons, fingerprinting, or any other tracking technologies. We do not track your browsing behaviour across our site or any other sites.

3. No Contact Forms or User Input

Our website does not contain any contact forms, comment sections, or features that allow you to submit personal information. The only way to contact us is via the email address on our Support page — a standard email link that uses your own email client.

4. No Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or social media plugins that could collect your data. Our website is purely informational and static.

5. External Links

Our website may contain links to other sites (e.g. email client, Google Play). If you click on a third-party link, you will be directed to that site. We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content or privacy practices of any third-party sites.

6. Children's Privacy

Our website does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. Since we collect no data at all, this clause is not practically applicable.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
