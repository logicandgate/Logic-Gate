❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I withdraw Tan to my Solana wallet? +

Go to the "Wallet" section in the app, tap "Withdraw", enter your Solana wallet address (must be a valid Solana address). Minimum withdrawal is 1 Tan. Withdrawals are processed within 48 hours.

My referral bonus is not showing. What do I do? +

Make sure your friend used your referral code during signup. Bonuses are credited when the referred friend is active with the app. If it's still missing, email us with both usernames, email addresses and UIDs.

I lost my progress after reinstalling. +

Progress is tied to your device ID. If you changed devices, please contact support with your old and new device identifiers — we can manually transfer your Tan balance. We strongly recommend linking an email in a future update.

Is my Solana wallet safe? Can you take my funds? +

No — we never have access to your private keys or wallet funds. We only send Solana to your wallet. Always keep your recovery phrase secure.

How do I delete my account and personal data? +

Send an email to support@logicandgate.com with "Delete my Tanzanite account" in the subject. We will remove your in-game data and any stored wallet addresses immediately.

Can I use multiple devices or accounts? +

No. For more information, read our Terms and Conditions (Tanzanite Mining).

Why are there so many ads? +

Sorry to hear that — but as you see, it's a reward-based ecosystem. For that reason we run ads to keep the service alive and pay out rewards.
