FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I withdraw TAN to my Solana wallet? +

Go to the Wallet section in the app, tap Withdraw, and enter a valid Solana wallet address. The minimum withdrawal is 1 TAN. Withdrawals are processed within 48 hours.

My referral bonus is not showing. +

Make sure your friend used your referral code during signup. Bonuses are credited once the referred friend is active. If it's still missing, email us with both usernames, email addresses and UIDs.

I lost my progress after reinstalling. +

Progress is tied to your device ID. If you changed devices, contact support with your old and new device identifiers — we can manually transfer your TAN balance. We strongly recommend linking an email in a future update.

Is my Solana wallet safe? +

Absolutely. We never have access to your private keys or wallet funds. We only send Solana to your wallet. Always keep your recovery phrase secure and offline.

How do I delete my account and data? +

Send an email to support@logicandgate.com with the subject "Delete my Tanzanite account". We will remove your in-game data and any stored wallet addresses immediately.

Can I use multiple devices or accounts? +

No. One account per user is strictly enforced. For the full policy, refer to our Terms and Conditions inside the Tanzanite Mining app.

Why are there so many ads? +

As a reward-based ecosystem, ads keep the service running and fund real TAN token payouts. We appreciate your understanding.
